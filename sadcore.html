Uli K

Uli K is a London-based singer, producer and member of the collective of artists called Bala Club. He has collaborated with GTBSG artists such as ECCO2K, Yung Lean, and Bladee.

ECCO2K

Zak Arogundade, better known by his stage name ECCO2K (Ecco), but also known as Aloegarten or g'LOSS, is an English/Swedish rapper, producer and designer from Stockholm, Sweden. Ecco is a member of GTBSG (Gravity Boys Shield Gang) and often collaborates with them on projects. His style mysterious due to the little amount of tracks he has released, but is definitive within the group.

Thaiboy Digital

Thanapat Thaothawong, better known by his stage name Thaiboy Digital is a Thai-Swedish rapper. His mixtape Tiger received a 5 in Swedish newspaper DN and has performed at the Royal Swedish Theater. Thaiboy Digital is a part of the Gravity Boys Shield Gang (GTBSG) with other artists such as Bladee and ECCO2K.

Bladee

Bladee is a rapper that’s associated with Yung Lean also from Stockholm, Sweden. His style of rapping usually involves melodic auto tune while spitting raps about his lifestyle and constant battle with depression. He’s also the CEO of his own company that is called 'Drain Gang' and is apart of the music group GTBSG.
